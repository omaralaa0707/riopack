About Riopack

A century of craft,
built in Egypt.

Riopack Co. began as an extension of Ashour Printing Press in 1926. What started as a regional printing operation grew into one of Egypt's most trusted names in rotogravure and offset packaging, serving both the local market and export destinations worldwide.

Our reach spans tea packaging, cigarette wraps, aluminum foil, medicine boxes, soap wrappers, food-grade laminates, and beyond. With graphic design and pre-press departments in-house, we translate client briefs into precise packaging outcomes at every stage.

"Pioneering excellence in printing and packaging: our vision guides every press, every cut, every fold."

Our equipment

Industrial-grade machinery

01
Rotogravure Presses
8 & 9 color Italian-made machines. Fully registered by eltromat and Bobst technology with electrostatic charge equipment for white-spot elimination.
02
Offset Machines
Heidelberg 2–4–6 colour presses. Bobst cutting & creasing. Hobst folder gluer. Hot foil stamping. German UV varnish coating. 2 Lamination machines.
03
Laminators & Multi-Coaters
Pagendram solvent base lamination (Germany). Nord Meccanica solvent-base and solventless machines (Italy) for double-layer food, dairy, and dry product packaging.
04
Slitters & Transformers
C-schiavi slitters in multiple sizes (Italy). Goebel slitters in multiple sizes (Germany).
05
Punching & Cone Making
Risen punching machines (Germany). 4 lange cone making machines (Germany) for ice cream cone production.
06
Extruder & Other Fields
PVC film production (8–150 microns). Polyethylene blowing film lines. Printed/treated aluminium and mix-pap covers for chocolate and yoghurt. Banderole role wrapper.
