Offset Products

Our Process
01
Prepress & Plates
Digital artwork verification, colour separation, and CTP plate making to ISO standard.
02
Offset Press Run
Heidelberg 2–6 colour presses with real-time spectrophotometer colour control on every sheet.
03
Foil · UV · Lamination
Hot foil stamping, spot UV varnish, and gloss or matte metallic lamination, all applied in-house.
04
Die-cutting & Gluing
Bobst precision die-cutting and Hobst folder-gluer assembly with 100% carton count verification.
